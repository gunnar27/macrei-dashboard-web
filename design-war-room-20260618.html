Design War Room

MacREI Portfolio Dashboard → Institutional Grade · June 18 2026 · 5 advisors + peer review + chairman

Verdict

Build the time dimension first (monthly Income/Expense/NOI trends + KPI sparklines), then per-building drill-down, then trust signals (last-synced, source, period-over-period deltas). Visual polish rides along — it is not the lever. Keep dark, one accent color, kill duplicate KPIs, demote the donut.

THE ONE THING FIRST: Add the time axis — a monthly Income / Expense / NOI trend chart (the GL data already has dates) plus sparklines on the KPI cards. Every advisor independently named the missing time dimension as the #1 gap.

Where the council agrees

No time dimension = #1 gap (unanimous). Snapshots are reporting; trends are analytics.
Drill-down required. Click a building → unit detail, lease expirations, rent vs market, WO aging, that asset's GL trend.
Sparklines in KPI cards · keep dark + one signal accent · faint grid, tabular numbers, conditional formatting.
Lease expiration view (30/60/90) — the only forward-looking view · delinquency aging by building.
Trust = audit trail: source attribution, "last synced" timestamp, period-over-period deltas on every figure.

Where the council clashes

IA: multi-view sidebar app + drill-down pages (4 advisors) vs. keep one scrolling page + slide-out modals at this scale (First Principles). Chairman: hybrid — keep tight overview, add per-building drill-down route, light nav grows with new views.
Sequencing: polish/ship-fast (Executor) vs. fix data model + context first (Contrarian). Peer review sided with data-first.

Blind spots peer review caught

Verify suspect numbers — Past Due ≈ a full month's rent; 0 work orders. (Both explained: WOs were all on off-boarded "10th"; past due is test-site cruft. Make numbers self-explaining so they pass a smell test.)
Role-based views / governance — LP vs operator exposure + permissions + lineage.
Mobile/responsive — does dark+dense survive a phone viewport?
